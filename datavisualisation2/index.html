Why Choose To Study In Melbourne

Visualisation by Kai Quan Ang

Home to some of the top universities in Australia

Melbourne is home to some of the top universities not only in Australia but the world! Monash University and The University of Melbourne are highly regarded for Engineering and Technology performance well above world standards.

They are ranked among the top 10 universities in Australia with The University of Melbourne sitting comfortably at the top of the ranks aand Monash University not far behind for academic reputation.

The campuses here in Melbourne also focus heavily on positive student experiences with institutions providing quality support services across accomodation, employment, legal as well as networking. This makes Melbourne an ideal destination for students all over the world.

High employment rate

Melbourne is the centre of technology commonly hailed as the technology capital of Australia. More than half of Australia's top 20 technology companies are based right here in Melbourne.

You can never dismiss the creative industries in Melbourne too as it has been shown to be a growing field here in Victoria along with others such as tourism and events, sustainability and new energy, and professional services like finance, insurance and engineering.

Some of our Victorian universities boasts an excellent score on employers reputation along with some of the highest employment outcome scores ensuring a bright and solid career path for its graduates.

All these growing industries is the reason why the start-up scene here in Melbourne is growing at a rapid rate. Along with the high employability of the universities you can rest assured that Melbourne is a city full of opportunities.

Multicultural and diverse population

The diverse and multicultural population of Melbourne is one of the reasons why it has been a popular study destination for students all over the world.

The vibrant city is home to over 140 different cultures from all corners of the world and has over 200 different languages spoken other than English here in Melbourne.

The map shows the diversity of the student populations as well as the faculty populations for each university here in Melbourne. It is easy to determine that on an average, Melbourne universities have a high international student and faculty ratio.

Students that choose to study in Melbourne have the opportunity of meeting new people from a variety of cultures as well as experience a range of cultural events and celebrations from all around the world.
